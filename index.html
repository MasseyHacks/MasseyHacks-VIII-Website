MasseyHacks VIII

May 14th-15th, 2022

Canada's oldest high school hackathon

MasseyHacks is a high school hackathon where 150+ students of all skill levels gather to learn, create, and experience something amazing. Over this 36-hour event, you will find seasoned mentors to learn from, lively activities to take part in, and a plethora of workshops to discover! Complete with three main learning streams based on your hacking experience, MasseyHacks is a place to explore the world of computer science and technology.

./FAQ

General

How do I apply?

Apply to MasseyHacks VIII here.

Who can participate?

MasseyHacks is available for all students in Grade 7 through Grade 12.

How much does it cost to sign up?

MasseyHacks is completely free! Sign up with your friends for the low cost of zero for the chance to win awesome prizes!

Do I need programming experience to participate?

Not at all! We’ll teach you the fundamentals of programming and mentors will be available to assist you. While MasseyHacks is a beginner-friendly hackathon, we highly recommend you participate in JumpStart to gain a deeper understanding of technical skills.

Jumpstart

Can I still earn prizes in JumpStart?

Yes! Gain points by attending workshops and completing projects to get sweet prizes through JumpStart. Sign up and get ready to start racking up those coins!

What format will JumpStart be held in?

JumpStart will take place fully virtually through our MasseyHacks Discord, with workshops taking place on Zoom.

MasseyHacks

What format will MasseyHacks be held in?

This year, MasseyHacks will be presented to you in an all new hybrid model. Hackers in the Greater Essex County District School Board have the opportunity to attend MasseyHacks in-person, taking place at Vincent Massey Secondary School. In-person participants will return home in the evening to continue working on their hacks and participating in virtual events! All other participants will attend the hackathon completely virtually. All our workshops and major events will be accessible to hackers attending in-person and virtually. Due to capacity limits, in-person spots for MasseyHacks are not guaranteed.

How many people can I have on my team?

Groups can have anywhere from 1 to 4 people. Teams can consist of in-person and virtual participants.

Will food be provided?

Yes, all your nutritional needs will be satisfied free of charge throughout our in-person MasseyHacks event.

What is being done to prevent the spread of COVID-19 at MasseyHacks?

Here at MasseyHacks, keeping hackers safe is and will always be our number one priority. Some steps we plan to take this year include splitting hackers into cohorts to make sure social distancing can be maintained, and regularly sanitizing all surfaces and hacker areas throughout the event. For more information on our COVID-19 policies, please refer to our Code of Conduct.

Do I need a team to participate?

Nope! Feel free to go in solo, but remember all good things are better together. We’ll also have team formation at the beginning of the event where you can unite with other hackers to form the dream team!

What do I need to bring?

Definitely consider bringing:

  • All your tech! - Bring laptops, phones, iPads, chargers, powerstrips and anything else you’ll need to create your hack
  • ID - bring a piece ofindentification for hacker sign-in

If you have any questions or would like to inform us of any issues, please email us at hello@masseyhacks.ca
